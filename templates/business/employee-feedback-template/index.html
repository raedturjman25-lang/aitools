Introduction

The Employee Feedback Template helps managers gather honest, structured feedback about work experience, performance, team culture, and support needs. It is useful for annual reviews, pulse surveys, and manager check-ins because it turns broad questions into clear, actionable prompts. The template helps avoid vague feedback by focusing on strengths, improvement areas, goals, and the support the employee needs to do their best work.
