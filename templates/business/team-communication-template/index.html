Meta Description

Team Communication Template to write clear internal messages, updates, and requests. Use this prompt to improve team coordination.

Introduction

The Team Communication Template helps teams share updates that are short, direct, and easy to act on. It works well for announcements, reminders, blockers, and cross-functional requests. The template reduces back-and-forth by structuring the message around the context, what changed, who owns the next step, and when the team should respond.

FAQ

Is a team communication template better than a full memo?

For quick updates, yes. It is shorter and easier to act on than a long memo.
