Meta Description

SWOT Analysis Template to map strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. Use this prompt to guide strategy and planning decisions.

Introduction

The SWOT Analysis Template helps teams evaluate a business, product, or initiative using a balanced view of internal and external factors. It is useful for strategic planning, leadership meetings, and launch preparation because it forces clear thinking about where the business is strong, where it is vulnerable, and where the market offers upside or risk. The template reduces vague brainstorming by asking for concrete evidence and action points in each quadrant.
