FAQ

What length should an SEO article be?

Article length depends on competition and topic depth; start with 1,200–1,800 words for mid-level topics and longer for comprehensive guides.

How many internal links should I include?

Include 3–8 relevant internal links depending on article length, prioritizing useful and contextual anchors.
