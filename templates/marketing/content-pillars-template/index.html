Introduction

The Content Pillars Template helps teams define 3–5 core topics that represent your brand’s authority and audience interest. Each pillar includes subtopics, content formats, keyword focus, and distribution ideas. This framework ensures every piece of content supports a pillar and contributes to long-term SEO and audience loyalty. Use the template to plan quarterly editorial calendars, repurpose assets, and brief writers with clear topic boundaries. It reduces scattered content efforts and focuses resources on topics that matter for search and conversions.
