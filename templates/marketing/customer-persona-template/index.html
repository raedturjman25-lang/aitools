Introduction

This Customer Persona Template helps marketing and product teams capture essential audience details in a single, repeatable format. It focuses on demographics, goals, pain points, decision criteria, information sources, and common objections. Personas help you tailor messaging, prioritize product features, and design better experiments. Use this template when you need to align messaging across campaigns, build targeted content, or brief creatives. The structured format reduces ambiguity and helps teams make decisions grounded in real user context rather than assumptions.
