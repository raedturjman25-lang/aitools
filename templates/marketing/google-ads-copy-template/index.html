The Template (Copy Section)

Role: Search ads specialist
Campaign type: [search/display/performance-max]
Primary keyword: [keyword]
Product/service: [product name]
Unique value: [what sets you apart]
Landing page focus: [primary conversion point]

Output:
1) 10 headline suggestions (30-char friendly)
2) 6 description options (90-char friendly)
3) 4 path text variations
4) Best-match keyword-landing copy pairing

Rules:
- Use keyword naturally in headlines when possible.
- Emphasize benefit or urgency in descriptions.
- Keep each line concise and action-focused.

How to Use This Template

  1. Insert your primary keyword and product details into the prompt.
  2. Run in your AI tool and review headlines for relevance and compliance.
  3. Pick top headlines and match them to high-intent descriptions.
  4. Ensure landing page mirrors the headline promise for quality score.

FAQ

Will AI respect ad policies?

AI can produce compliant copy, but you must review ads for platform policy restrictions and claims. Always validate claims and ensure landing pages match the ad content.

How do I keep headlines within limits?

Use the character guidance in the output and trim adjectives; the template generates headlines tailored to common length limits.

Can this be used for dynamic search ads?

Yes—generate a pool of headlines and descriptions to feed into dynamic ad groups for variation testing.

Is keyword insertion recommended?

Only when it enhances clarity. Overuse can make copy awkward; prefer natural phrasing that includes the keyword.
