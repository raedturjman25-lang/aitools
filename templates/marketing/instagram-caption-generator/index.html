Introduction

Use it to maintain a consistent voice while testing different hooks and CTAs across your feed and stories.

FAQ

Should I always use hashtags?

Use a small set of relevant hashtags to aid discovery, but prioritize the hook and CTA; over-tagging can look spammy.

How long should an Instagram caption be?

Keep primary hooks short; longer captions can work for storytelling but should be scannable with line breaks and bullets.
