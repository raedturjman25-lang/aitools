For affiliates, this means less time building and more time promoting — if you follow best practices.

Who it's for

Step-by-step setup (practical guide)

The following steps will get your funnel live and optimized.

  1. Purchase and access: Use the vendor link to register and claim the funnel package. Save your login and FTP/CMS instructions.
  2. Deploy funnel pages: Follow the included guide to upload pages to your hosting or import into the provided funnel builder. Test form submissions and tracking pixels.
  3. Connect an autoresponder: Paste the provided sequences into your email platform, update sender details, and enable automation.
  4. Set up tracking: Add your analytics and affiliate tracking IDs to the dashboard and preview funnels in incognito to verify conversions.
  5. Customize creatives: Replace placeholder logos, adapt headlines for your audience, and localize the copy where needed.
  6. Launch soft: Begin with a small paid test or organic promotion to collect initial data, then iterate headlines and lead magnets.

Conversion tips & tactical advice

To get the most from DFY funnels, follow these recommendations:

  • Use short, attention-grabbing headlines and A/B test them.
  • Promote with native content (short video + link) for higher engagement.
  • Warm audiences with value-first emails before sending the main funnel.
  • Leverage the included swipe files — adapt, don't copy verbatim.
  • Track metrics: CR, CTR, and revenue per visitor to optimize ROI.
